Chatbot Playbooks

Virtual Legal Assistant for Law Firms

  • The Problem

Attorneys are often tied up with legal payable work and unable to answer calls, resulting in lost leads and a decline in client satisfaction.

  • The Outcome

We extended the 24/7 virtual receptionist chatbot app, by catering playbooks ensuring the collection of all necessary legal details for law firms. Lawyers reported a 57% less of interruptions 63% increase in lead capture and conversion in just 3 weeks after implementation.

  • Background

The 24/7 legal assistance also help attorneys to handle client intake, book consultations, collect payments, and manage customer interactions, ensuring that every call is answered instantly and professionally.

  • The solution
  • 4 tech team
  • 1 CEO

The Decision to create tailored Chatbot Playbooks for Attorneys stemmed from a deep dive into our user analytics. By analyzing user patterns, we discovered a significant insight: a large portion of our client base consisted of law firms. This revelation prompted us to focus on developing a solution specifically designed for legal professionals. We recognized that attorneys faced unique challenges in client intake and case evaluation processes. By leveraging our existing chatbot technology and customizing it for legal use cases, we saw an opportunity to address these specific needs.

The chatbot guides users through a series of targeted questions, ensuring the collection of all necessary legal details like case type, timeline, and personal information. My role involved designing intuitive conversation flows, integrating decision trees to adjust the dialogue based on user responses, and ensuring a seamless, user-friendly interface. This helped lawyers reduce intake time and improve client qualification accuracy, resulting in more efficient case evaluations.
